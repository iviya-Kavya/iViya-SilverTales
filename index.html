Our Story

Born from nature’s rhythm, Iviya crafts silver jewellery that reads like a poem. We blend traditional silversmithing with delicate organic forms—leaves, moonlit curves, and tide‑worn textures—to create pieces that feel both ancestral and contemporary.

Handcrafted

Each piece is lovingly forged by hand using ethically sourced silver and time-honoured techniques.

Nature Inspired

Designs celebrate botanical forms, lunar motifs, and flowing lines—bringing the outdoors into wearable art.

Limited Editions

Small-batch collections ensure rarity and attention to detail—pieces that feel like personal talismans.
